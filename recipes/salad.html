Salad

Salad is a healthy dish enjoyed by many vegetarians and folks on a diet. It consist of several plants mixed together and served cold.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Cherry Tomatoes
  • Shredded cheese
  • Salad dressing
  • Croutons

Directions

  1. Place all the ingredients inside a large mixing bowl
  2. Toss the ingredients vigoroulsy for 1 minute
  3. Serve in a small bowl
