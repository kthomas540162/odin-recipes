Pizza

Pizza is the comfort food of America and a staple of casual cuisine. Found in a variety of styles and topped with numerous meats and vegetables to match any palate.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Red tomato sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Sliced pepperoni
  1. Spread Pizza dough accross a round baking sheet
  2. Spread a thin layer of the red tomato sauce accross the pizza dough leaving just the outside edges of the dough without sauce
  3. Sprinkle the shredded cheese over the sauce unitl lightly covered
  4. Apply pepperoni over the cheese to leaving small gaps inbetween each peice of pepperoni
  5. Place Pizza in the oven at 350 degrees ferinheight for 15 minutes
  6. Remove pizza from over and allow it to cool for 5 minutes before slicing and serving
